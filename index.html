Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to repair my phone?

A: The repair time depends on the type of issue. Simple repairs like screen replacements may take around 1-2 hours, while more complex issues could take longer. Our technicians will provide you with an estimated time when you drop off your phone.

Q: What types of phones do you repair?

A: We specialize in repairing a wide range of phones, including iPhones, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, and more. Feel free to contact us if you have a specific model or brand-related question.

Q: Is it worth repairing an older phone?

A: It depends on the extent of the damage and the value of your phone. Our technicians can assess the condition and provide you with an honest recommendation. In some cases, repairing an older phone can be a cost-effective solution compared to buying a new one.

Q: Do you provide a warranty for your repairs?

A: Yes, we offer a limited warranty on our repairs. The warranty duration may vary depending on the type of repair. Please check with our staff for specific warranty details related to your repair.

Q: Can I get a quote before the repair?

A: Absolutely! We provide free quotes for all our services. You can visit our store for an in-person assessment, or you can contact us online by providing details about your phone and the issue you're facing. We'll get back to you with a quote promptly.
